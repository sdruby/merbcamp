MerbCamp is the first official gathering for the Merb community.

The conference will take place in sunny San Diego on October 11 - 12, on the campus of UCSD, the home of San Diego Ruby.

We’re charging $50 for MerbCamp.

Our goal is to keep the registration fee low since we realize a lot of folks will be traveling to San Diego from far away.

Your registration fee includes access to all talks and events, two lunches, one dinner, a conference t-shirt, plus a few other surprises.

Have any questions? Check the FAQ.

MerbCamp is happening at Calit2’s Atkinson Hall.

  • Ethernet and power drops in theater seats? Check.
  • Live webcast of MerbCamp talks? Check.
